What is Vegetation?
Vegetation refers to the plant life covering a region. It includes forests, grasslands, crops, and all forms of plant communities that shape our ecosystems.
Vegetation Index (NDVI)
The Normalized Difference Vegetation Index (NDVI) measures plant health using satellite imagery. It ranges from -1 to +1, where higher values indicate healthier, denser vegetation.
  • +0.7 to +0.9 Dense, healthy forests
  • +0.4 to +0.7 Moderate vegetation
  • +0.2 to +0.4 Sparse vegetation
  • -0.1 to +0.2 Bare soil or desert
